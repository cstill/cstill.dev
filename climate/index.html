Our Mission

Show Effects

We want to showcase the detrimental effects of climate change to the masses, in an effort to encourage those on the fence regarding the issue to be inspired to make a difference.

Inspire Change

We believe that one of the major hurdles in making a difference with regards to climate change is a lack of direction on how an individual can make and inspire change on their own. We want to change that, by showcasing ways in which anyone can help.

Grow Further

Our team is committed to outreach from a multi-modal standpoint. From videos to poetry, we can ensure that our message reaches far and wide, capturing the attention of as many individuals as possible.

  • Increases in Droughts and Heat Waves

    Precipitation is crucial to our survival as humanity - climate change is going to cause us a lack of access to this critical resource. Without precipitation, modern agriculture becomes much harder to manage. For small-scale farmers, providing locally grown fresh produce to customers could become a thing of the past.

  • Rising Sea Levels

    NASA has observed an 8 inch rise of the sea level on our home planet since 1880. The primary cause for this increase is the melting of large ice clusters in our ocean. Our beloved Southern California beaches could soon meet their demise and sea levels rise and engourage more flooding and erosion in nearby areas.

  • More Wildfires

    We are no strangers to wildfires here in California, and we know how devastating they can be on the planet and those who are unfortunate enough to have a home within their area of effect. Because of increased temperatures, heat waves, and droughts, wildfires will have a much easier time spreading and destroying anything which lies in their path.
